Our Mission

Our economic, political, and social activities are largely based on modern communication and IT infrastructure. These infrastructures are naturally security-critical, and their misuse by malicious actors or even careless users can be disastrous.

Our mission is:

  • Contribute to foundations, methods, and tools for building secure systems.
  • Advance methods and tools for validating system security, i.e., rigorous approaches to modeling, testing, verifying, and formally certifying the security of IT systems.
  • Apply our results to societally relevant problems, such as network protocols, new Internet architectures, identity management, payment and financial processing systems, and related domains.
  • Educate students and researchers in Information Security and related areas.
  • Work with partners from industry, government agencies, and the public at large.

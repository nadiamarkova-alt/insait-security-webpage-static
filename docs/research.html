Research Areas

Our group focuses on cutting-edge research in multiple areas of information security

Privacy and Data Protection

Formal change impact analysis of privacy policies, data protection regulations, and privacy-preserving technologies to ensure compliance and safeguard user data.

Formal Verification of Protocols and Systems

Verifying the security properties of cryptographic protocols, secure multi-party computation, and distributed systems using formal methods and model checking techniques.

AI and Security

Investigating the use of Large Language Models (LLMs) for security applications, including adversarial attacks, defenses, and the development of secure AI systems.
